ClothesCast — your daily one-sentence weather brief.

Every morning at the time you choose, ClothesCast posts a single, actionable sentence:

• "4°C warmer than yesterday — leave the jumper at home."
• "50% chance of rain at 3pm — take an umbrella."

No more squinting at hourly charts before you're properly awake. ClothesCast compares yesterday's actual weather with today's forecast, applies the wardrobe rules you've configured, and tells you what's changed and what to do about it.

ONE SENTENCE, GENERATED FOR TODAY

The brief is written by Google Gemini using today's apparent ("feels-like") temperature, wind, precipitation timing, and any severe weather alerts in your area — not raw air temperatures. It speaks to the conditions you'll actually experience stepping outside, framed against yesterday so you know what to add or drop versus what you wore.

WEAR IT, HEAR IT, OR BOTH

Pick a delivery mode that suits your morning:
• Notification — a normal Android push with the brief.
• Voice — ClothesCast speaks the brief aloud through the platform text-to-speech engine, optionally upgraded to a Gemini voice for a more natural read. Useful as an audible wake-up that doesn't need the screen on.
• Both, when you want a notification you can also re-read.

WARDROBE RULES THAT MATCH YOUR KIT

Configure thresholds that map weather to the clothes you actually own — "jumper below 12 °C", "gloves below 5 °C", "umbrella above 40% rain". The brief speaks in your wardrobe's vocabulary, not in generic icons.

CALENDAR-AWARE (OPTIONAL)

Opt in to read today's calendar, and ClothesCast can pin advice to specific events: "Bring an umbrella for your 3pm park run." Only event titles, times, and locations are read, and only on-device — nothing is uploaded.

SEVERE WEATHER ALERTS

When a significant warning lands for your area (storm, flood, heat advisory), a separate high-priority notification fires on a dedicated channel — independent of your morning brief.

TODAY SCREEN

Open the app any time for an hourly chart of temperature and feels-like across the day, plus a multi-model confidence badge showing how much the major forecast models (ECMWF, GFS, ICON) agree on today's apparent high and peak rainfall. When they disagree, you know to check again later.

ROBUST SCHEDULING

The morning brief uses Android's exact-alarm primitive and re-arms automatically across reboots, timezone changes, and locale changes — about as reliable as the platform allows. On aggressive OEMs (some Xiaomi, Oppo, OnePlus and Samsung profiles) you may need to allow ClothesCast to run in the background; see dontkillmyapp.com for per-OEM steps.

PRIVACY

Forecast data comes from Open-Meteo — no account, no key. The brief is generated by Gemini using your own API key (bring-your-own-key), encrypted on-device with Android Keystore. Calendar reading is opt-in and stays on-device. Location is whichever city you pick in Settings.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

• Android 8.0 or later.
• A free Gemini API key from aistudio.google.com.
• Notification permission (Android 13+).

IDEAL FOR

People who want one clear answer in the morning instead of decoding a forecast — commuters, parents getting kids out the door, dog walkers, runners and cyclists.
